Contingencies

Weather swaps & local notes

Rainy-Day Swaps

  • Bukchon/Insadong walking (Day 2) → National Museum of Korea or COEX indoor mall
  • Seongsu-dong browsing (Day 4A) → Starfield Library + COEX underground mall
  • Gwangjang Market (Day 6) is fine in light rain — covered alleys. N Seoul Tower cable car may pause in high wind; indoor fallback is Lotte World Tower's Seoul Sky

Local Tips & Traps

  • Carry cash for Gwangjang Market and pocha tents — cards aren't always accepted
  • Myeongdong is very safe at night; in Hongdae/Itaewon later on, stick to main streets
  • Skip pushy street "tea ceremony" touts near Insadong — overpriced tourist trap
  • Book Gyeongbokgung's free English guided tour on arrival at Heungnyemun Gate — no reservation needed under 10 people
  • DMZ tour operators can cancel same-day for military drills — don't schedule anything tight right after
